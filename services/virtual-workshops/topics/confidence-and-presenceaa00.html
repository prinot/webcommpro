Confidence and presence

Uplift your world by learning ways to build your confidence and presence.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • A 100-minute learning journey
  • Live and interactive
  • Available on multiple platforms
  • Ideal for 4-12 participants
  • PDF playbook included
  • Evidence-based training for stellar learning outcomes

Confidence and presence are inseparably linked.

When we feel confident, we’re in the best position to manage our level of presence. Which is essential as different situations call for specific types of presence. Get it right and, you'll master any interaction, get it wrong and, your message will fall flat.

This workshop helps you to take control of your presence by building a bulwark of confidence.

This workshop covers how to:

  • Identify how confidence levels let us control whether we adopt a higher or lower level of presence
  • Recognize what helps and hinders confidence levels in different situations
  • Build a more confident mindset
  • Apply the learning in the real world with practical tips on how to control and build presence
