Your complete virtual Train the Trainer program

The Virtual Training Team Train the Trainer Program consists of 6 linked workshops.
Here they are in more detail.

DELIVERY MASTERCLASS

100+ tips and techniques to help you make the transition from the classroom to the virtual training world.

DESIGN MASTERCLASS

Everything you need to know to virtualise your classroom content, and design new material from the ground up.

INTERACTION MASTERCLASS

Learn our tried and tested method to create a psychologically safe environment to get conversations going.

PLATFORM MASTERCLASS

Play, learn and master the advanced features of the platform of your choice.

PRACTICAL MASTERCLASS #1

Learn by doing and make mistakes in a safe environment as you design and deliver a short virtual workshop.

PRACTICAL MASTERCLASS #2

Use all the feedback from the previous session to refine and redeliver your workshop, then explore ideas and get excited about your virtual training future.
