Supporting Your Team to Thrive in the ‘New Normal’

A webinar exploring how to develop your L&D approach to support your team to excel in a post-Covid hybrid world.

27th April 2021 | 14:00 BST
Zoom Meetings

Highlight 1

Get an introduction to the hot topics in virtual training right now.

Highlight 2

Learn how to best support your team in continuing to thrive in a virtual environment.

Highlight 3

Gain insight into wellbeing, assertiveness, resilience, time management and coping with change.

It’s Time to Thrive!

The world has gone virtual. One year in and we’re beginning to settle into the new landscape, but many of us are still focusing on “survive” rather than “thrive”.

However, as restrictions begin to lift and we start to look toward a post-Covid world, it seems more and more likely that remote and hybrid working is here to stay.

This interactive virtual event will help you to identify how you can support your team in continuing to excel in a virtual environment, and offer an insight into the virtual training support we can offer.

Key Topics

  • Wellbeing

  • Assertiveness

  • Resilience

  • Change

  • Time Management

Wellbeing in the virtual world

As we adjust to an increasingly virtual world, it is more important than ever that we work in a way that safeguards both physical and psychological wellbeing. Gain an insight into how stress affects us and learn some practical steps you can take to overcome this.

Assertiveness for everyone

Assertiveness at work can be tricky- even more so when we’re physically separated from our team. Understand some of the common barriers to assertion and learn some practical approaches to being appropriately assertive.

Bullet-proof resilience

In our ever-changing and often uncertain current climate, personal resilience has never been more important. Discover why we sometimes respond negatively to challenging situations and begin to develop a toolkit of techniques to help build resilience.

Change happens, let’s deal with it

Navigating workplace change can be difficult, especially when working as part of a remote team. Understand how people respond to change and begin to discover the benefits of a change-fit mindset.

Time management for when you’re time poor

Most of us are busier than ever, both at work and in our personal lives. Establishing the discipline and routines to help with focus, delivery and connectivity has never been more important. Learn how to manage your time even when you feel like you just don’t have any!

About The Virtual Training Team

Coach-led, virtual training is the future. Our founders, John and Catherine, saw this coming years ago!

In 2015, they used their 10+ years of virtual training expertise to develop the Virtual Training Team (VTT) to help companies and trainers transition to virtual quickly and confidently.

5 years later, this is still the VTT mission!

We know that making the switch to virtual training isn't easy. There's technology to get to grips with, a whole new dynamic when coaching through a computer screen, and the challenges of converting face-to face content into a format that works in the virtual world. We are here to help.

We’ve been at the forefront of virtual training for over a decade and have delivered to people in over 70 countries. Some of our coaches have well over 10,000 hours of virtual training delivery under their belts. This means you’re in safe hands.

Your success is our success, so we’ll do whatever it takes to get you over the line.
